The Problem

Vendors were forced to maintain separate inventories for Amazon, Flipkart, and their own websites. This led to overselling, manual data entry, and constant price mismatches, costing time and revenue.

2

Our Solution

We built a unified inventory dashboard with real‑time sync across all marketplaces. An AI‑powered pricing engine suggests optimal prices, and automated order routing prevents overselling.

Development Process

1. Research & Discovery

User interviews with 15 vendors, competitor analysis, and feature prioritization workshop.

2. UI/UX Design

Low‑fidelity wireframes → high‑fidelity prototypes → usability testing with real vendors.

3. Agile Development

Sprints of 2 weeks, CI/CD pipeline, automated testing. Core features: inventory sync, dashboard, AI pricing.

4. Launch & Iterate

Soft launch with 10 beta vendors, collected feedback, performance optimization, then public release.

Results

  • 70% reduction in inventory management time
  • 40% increase in sales within first quarter
  • Zero overselling incidents since launch
  • 50+ active vendors onboarded organically
