About Me

Founder · Developer · 3D Designer · Entrepreneur

Building the Future, One Innovation at a Time

I'm Bibek Patra — a founder, developer, and creative technologist from Mayurbhanj, Odisha. I build digital products that merge design, intelligence, and purpose.
