Chitt Vijay – The Journey of Mastering the Mind

June 30, 2026 Bibek Patra

"मन पर विजय की संवाद यात्रा" – this book is not just a collection of thoughts, but a deep conversation about self‑mastery. Writing it was a transformative experience, and in this post, I share the behind‑the‑scenes journey of how this book came to life.

The Inspiration

During my own struggles with self‑discipline and mental clarity, I realized that true victory lies not outside, but within. The concept of "Chitt Vijay" (Victory of the Mind) emerged from ancient wisdom combined with modern practical insights.

The Writing Process

I spent over six months crafting each chapter. Early mornings were my best creative hours. I used BibekBook (my own publishing platform) to manage drafts, edits, and eventually publish. The goal was to create a book that speaks directly to the reader's inner battle.

Key Themes

The book explores topics like controlling thoughts, building resilience, and finding purpose. It's written in simple Hindi so that it reaches every person who needs it.

Why You Should Read It

If you've ever felt lost, demotivated, or overwhelmed, this book will guide you back to your center. It's a conversation that can change your perspective.
